It is four years since the events of Peru. Since their adventure
with Jackson Elias, the investigators have corresponded from
time-to-time with their good friend, who has continued to
travel the globe in pursuit of weird and dangerous cults. In
early January 1925, the investigators receive an intriguing
radiogram from Elias, from a ship at sea (Carlyle Papers:
The Expedition #1). As with all handouts, Keepers may find
it useful to prepare such material ahead of time, and to pass
out the copies when appropriate.
