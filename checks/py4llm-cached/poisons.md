# Sample Poisons 

|**Poison**|**Speed**|**Efect (1 dose)**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|---|
|Amanita (agaric<br>mushrooms)|6 to 24<br>hours|Violent stomach pains, vomiting,<br>jaundice.<br>1D10 damage|Responsible for 95% of fatalities from mushroom<br>poisoning, the death cap mushroom being<br>foremost.|

|Cobra Venom|15 to 60<br>minutes|Convulsions, respiratory failure.<br>4D10 damage|Contact with eyes will cause immediate irritation<br>and, if untreated, blindness. If serum is available<br>then major injury and death can be avoided.|

|Rattlesnake<br>Venom|6 to 24<br>hours|Vomiting, violent spasms, yellowish<br>vision.<br>2D10 damage|Venom destroys tissue, causing necrosis,<br>although rarely fatal (especially if antidote is<br>given within 1 to 2 hours).|
|Rohypnol|15 to 30<br>minutes|Unconsciousness for 4-8 hours,<br>possible amnesia.<br>No damage.|Odorless, colorless and only available in modern<br>game settings.|
|Strychnine|10 to 20<br>minutes|Violent muscle contractions,<br>asphyxiation, death.<br>4D10 damage|Death within hours if medical treatment not<br>provided.|
